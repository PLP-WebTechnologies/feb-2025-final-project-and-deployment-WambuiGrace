Dune

by Frank Herbert

Sci-Fi Fantasy

The story follows Paul Atreides, heir to House Atreides, as his family is betrayed by their rivals, the Harkonnens, with the support of the Emperor. Forced into exile, Paul finds refuge among the Fremen, the desert-dwelling natives of Arrakis. As he embraces their ways, he discovers his destiny as Muad’Dib, a messianic figure prophesied to lead the Fremen in a revolution against their oppressors.

The Hobbit

by John Ronald Reuel Tolkien

Adventure Fantasy

The Hobbit by J.R.R. Tolkien is a fantasy adventure novel that follows Bilbo Baggins, a humble hobbit who is unexpectedly drawn into a quest to reclaim a lost treasure from the fearsome dragon Smaug. Guided by the wizard Gandalf and accompanied by Thorin Oakenshield and his band of dwarves, Bilbo embarks on a perilous journey across Middle-earth. Along the way, he encounters trolls, goblins, elves, and the mysterious creature Gollum, from whom he acquires a magical ring that grants invisibility. Through courage and cleverness, Bilbo proves himself far more capable than he ever imagined, ultimately playing a crucial role in the fate of the dwarves and their homeland.

Non-fiction Self-help Psychology

Atomic Habits by James Clear is a practical guide to habit formation and personal improvement. The book emphasizes the power of small, consistent changes—or "atomic habits"—that compound over time to create significant results. Clear introduces the Four Laws of Behavior Change.
